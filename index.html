What is coaching

Coaching is helping someone through the desire to change.

A coach can help you improve your effectiveness and performance, focus your priorities so that you are not derailed by the day to day and help you overcome what is holding you back from living the life you want.

If you answer yes to any of the below questions, then coaching may just be what you are looking for:

  • Are you in a job that you are not enjoying or is not filfulling you?
  • Do you feel like you have missed out on the career that you wanted?
  • Do you want a change but you are not sure what?
  • Do you feel that you are not getting the recognition you deserve?
  • Does it feel like everyone else is doing better than you are?

How it works

Initial session

The first step is to have an introductory call to understand your requirements. This call lasts approx 30 minutes and is free of charge

Regular sessions

Coaching sessions typically last between 45 - 60 minutes, and are scheduled as you need them. Sessions can be face to face, by phone or skype. Sessions are held in the evening and on weekends (other times available on request).
